Our Story

For over two decades, our reputation as pioneers of innovative, bespoke design has earned us interior design assignments and development management projects around the world. We have created stunning aesthetics for lavish homes, yachts, and a wide range of commercial environments.

Conceived as a talent incubator, DeHub operates as one of the leading companies in Europe in the management of architects, interior designers, and product designers. DeHub, an active strategist, prides itself on fully accompanying its clients throughout the creative process.
